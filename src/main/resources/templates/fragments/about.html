My Background

Developer by skills

I’m comfortable using tools like IntelliJ IDEA, Maven, Tomcat, and Git, and I always aim to write clean, maintainable code that aligns with best practices.

In addition to my technical abilities, I bring strong soft skills like clear communication, teamwork, and problem-solving. I enjoy collaborating with others, whether it’s discussing technical challenges or aligning on user experience goals. I’m organized, focused, and able to manage deadlines effectively, even under pressure. I also value feedback and use it as a way to continuously grow in both skill and mindset.

What drives me most is the satisfaction of building something useful and reliable. I don’t just see development as a task, but as a creative process that brings ideas to life. I’m always eager to learn new things, take on challenges, and contribute to projects that make a difference. My goal is to grow as a developer while delivering quality and value through every line of code I write.
